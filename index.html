How It Works

Hire Options

Choose the option that works best for you

Option 1

Regular Hire

Our full range of wetsuits, surfboards and accessories available Thursday-Monday April through June and September through October, and every day in July and August. Set collection and return times.

  1. Book your hire
  2. Receive a link to fill out our registration form
  3. Meet us at the beach to collect your hire
  4. Enjoy your surf!

Option 2

Self-Service Hire at Cullen

A select range of wetsuits, surfboards and accessories that can be picked up and dropped off any time 365 days a year.

  1. Book your hire
  2. Receive a link to fill out our registration form
  3. Receive a code for accessing your kit from our equipment locker at the beach
  4. Enjoy your surf!

Hire Safety

Your safety is our top priority

RIP Current Safety

Know how to identify and safely navigate rip currents when surfing. Always stay calm and swim parallel to the shore if caught in a rip.

Surf Etiquette

Respect other surfers in the water. Learn proper surf etiquette including priority rules, paddling out, and sharing waves safely.
